**Time commitment:** ~30-45 min/day. Batch content on weekends.

## Key Rules:
1. **80/20 rule:** 80% value, 20% promotion. Lead with knowledge, not sales.
2. **Reply to EVERY comment** in the first 2 weeks. Engagement = visibility.
3. **Don't spam links.** Share the link once per post. Let the content sell.
4. **Track what works.** If Reddit drives more traffic than Facebook, go harder on Reddit.
5. **Be yourself.** Your authenticity — 16 years, real cases, real photos — is your marketing. Nobody else has this.
